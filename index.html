What We Do

Malaysia Low Voltage Switchboard Manufacturer

Radiant Options Sdn Bhd is a ISO 9001: 2015 accredited Low Voltage Switchboard Manufacturer which is based in Sarawak, Malaysia. The team is committed to provide different engineering solutions for designing, fabricating and customization of switchboards and control panels for customers. With over 20 years of engineering experience in this manufacturing industry, the team are able to manufacture low voltage switchboard up to 6000A. The team is equipped with modern facilities and highly trained staff for manufacturing process of switchboards. Besides, Radiant Options Sdn Bhd has ability to supply electrical parts from a range of local and international suppliers such as Schneider, ABB, LS, Mikro, Delab and others. These electrical parts will meet standard and requirements of EIU Sarawak, Sarawak Energy Berhad and JKR Malaysia. The team is committed to manufacture the most suitable and cost-effective low voltage switchboard to match the requirements of every customer.
